Exploratory Data Analysis

Class Distribution

The first step towards EDA is to check the distribution of various class in order to examine whether the dataset is suffering from imbalance or not? Based on the class distribution plot we can see clear imbalance with No-Anomaly being the majority class. This gives a clear indication that accuracy is not a good metric for evaluation instead we can choose class-wise accuracy or F1-score for model performance calculation.

Image type and Characteristics

Cell

Hot spot occurring with square geometry in single cell.

Cell-Multi

Hot spots occurring with square geometry in multiple cells.

Cracking

Module anomaly caused by cracking on module surface.

Hot-Spot

Hot spot on a thin film module.

Hot-Spot-Multi

Multiple hot spots on a thin film module.

Shadowing

Sunlight obstructed by vegetation, man-made structures, or adjacent rows.

Diode

Activated bypass diode, typically 1/3 of module.

Diode-Multi

Multiple activated bypass diodes, typically affecting 2/3 of module.

Vegetation

Panels blocked by vegetation.

Soiling

Dirt, dust, or other debris on surface of module.

Offline-Module

Entire module is heated.

No-Anomaly

Nominal solar module.
