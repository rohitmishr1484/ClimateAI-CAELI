AI in Renewable

LCOE

Levelized cost of energy (LCOE) is the common metric based on which different conventional or unconventional energy generating sources are compared from an economic perspective. In recent years solar or renewable energy sources although are intermittent but have lower LCOE as compared to coal-based generation.

Predictive Maintenance

From 2010 to 2019 solar has drawn the attention of investors with EUR 122 billion investment that amounts to 42.5% of all new renewable energy investment in Europe. System optimization plays a significant role in making global acceptance of solar on the utility-scale. Predictive maintenance acts as an accelerator to operate solar farms efficiently with less manual intervention and AI will certainly lead this effort.
